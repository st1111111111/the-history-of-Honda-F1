The history of Honda F1

The Histroy Of Honda F1

The power of dreams.

Nowadays, the car brand name Honda is quite familiar around the world. This company has been trying to do motorsports races since 1964. Honda Soichiro worked at a repair shop until he was 22 years old. Then He started his repair shop while making a racing car by himself. After that, at the age of 1948, the company of Honda was born in Hamamatsu Shizuoka Japan. In 1954, Honda has joined the international motorcycle sports race in Sao Paulo brazil which was the first time joined the race from a Japanese company. Then Honda had gotten first victory at the Isle of man UK TT race in 1961. After the victory, Honda tried to make a motorsports car then start the history of the Honda F1 in 1962.

Term 1

1964 - 1968

The history of Honda started from here. In 1963 they made their prototype f1 car which was RA270.

Term 2

1983 - 1992

Since term 1, Honda has changed many things in the company such as sales, and products. Also, work stuff as well. They tried to start from the F2 in 1983.

Term 3

2000 - 2008

Honda is back in 2000 with a young f1 team which was B.A.R (British American Racing) since 1992.

Term 4

2015 - current

Back to the F1 in 2015. Honda got the partnership with Maclaren then, starting the hard seasons.
