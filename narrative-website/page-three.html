Timeline

2000 - 2008

  • BAR HONDA 006

    In 1998, Honda announced to back the F1 circus. So, they backed F1 in 2000 with a young racing team which is B.A.R (British American Racing). Honda was an engine supplier while developing the car frame as well. However, since term 2 the Formula 1 industry has dramatically developed. Honda struggled to build a competitive car. They took five years to make a fast machine which was still relatively not good enough.

  • BAR HONDA 007

    In 2004, B.A.R Honda was a good season because of two good drivers who were Jenson Alexander Lyons Button and Takuma Sato. In 2004 B.A.R Honda got a constructor 2nd place. In next year was a terrible and tough season for B.A.R Honda. Button only one time got a pole position then Takuma was ranking 23rd.

  • MP4-30

    In 2006, Honda has applied for F1 through the new team with Jenson Button and Rubens Barrichello (a Brazilian Formula 1 driver). This team's name was SUPER AGURI F1 TEAM. Then In Hungary's grand Prix, Jenson button brought the win for Honda. Then, in 2007 a new machine RA107 with the same drivers Honda continued to join the F1 but, quite hard to get points. Term 3 was thought season for Honda F1.
