Term One

Timeline

1964-1968.

  • RA270

    In 1963 Honda made their first Formula one car called the RA270. This machine was made by the team the Honda motorcycle race. The name RA270 has many meanings. First of all, R is Racing. Secondly, A is an Automobile, and 270 means, this car tries to max speed is over 270km. Also, this car was completed in only a year. They planned to make RA270 from August 1962 to December 1963.

  • RA271

    Honda was supposed to supply the RA270 to Lotus (a British motorsports company) But, In January 1964 Lotus canceled the contract with Honda. Therefore, Honda had gotten a bit of time to join the F1. They updated RA270 to RA271. RA271 had updated the whole thing. Engine, chassis, and monocoque. This machine had 220hp which was quite high power compared to other f1 cars in the 1960s.

  • RA300

    In 1965 Honda was getting the first victory at the F1 Mexico Grand Prix by RA272. Since then, Honda had been struggling to win because of the heavy weight of the car. Because of this, Honda tried to learn How to make a lightweight machine from the UK. So, Honda got contracts with John Surtees (a British racing driver) and Lola Cars International (a British racing car contractor company). Then in 1967 F1 Italy grand Prix, they got a second victory.
